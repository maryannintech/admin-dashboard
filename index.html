Super Cool Project

Vertical Garden Project: This project involves creating a garden that grows vertically instead of horizontally. This is accomplished by installing a structure, such as a trellis or wall-mounted planter, and planting a variety of plants that can grow upwards, such as climbing vines, flowers, and vegetables.

Less Cool Project

Traditional Raised Bed Garden: This project involves creating a garden by constructing a raised bed using wood, bricks, or other materials. The raised bed is then filled with soil and planted with a variety of plants.

Impossible Project

The Floating Garden Project: This project involves creating a garden that floats above the ground. The garden is suspended in the air by a network of helium balloons, which are tethered to the ground.

Easy Peasy Project

Container Garden Project: This project involves creating a garden using containers, such as pots, hanging baskets, or window boxes. The containers can be filled with a variety of plants, such as flowers, herbs, or vegetables.

The More the Merrier Project

Community Garden Project: This project involves creating a garden space that is open to the community and managed by a group of volunteers. The garden can be used to grow food, flowers, or other plants, and can be a great way to bring people together and promote community involvement.
